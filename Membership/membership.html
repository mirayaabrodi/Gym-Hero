Questions & Answers

How does the 14-day trial work?

If you select a certain workout course of GYM, you can take an
opportunity to try it before paying the full price for it. To start the
trial, you have to be a registered user of our website and select a
training course

Can I change/cancel plans anytime?

After your training course begins, there are two ways of changing or
canceling your membership. If you are a new member owning a
Starter plan, you can switch to another plan only in half a year. Basic
and Pro members can switch to the plan of their choice instantly.
Canceling is accessible for all our members regardless of
their training package.

What happens at the end of my trial?

After your trial ends, you can pay for your favorite workout course
you selected before using cash or credit card. However, if you choose
a different course or workout package, you won’t be able to try it for
free before buying. If you have already been our loyal member for a
year or more, you also get permanent discounts on our membership
plans.

Do you offer discounts for students?

We care about the health of youth, and that’s why our managers
have developed a discount program for students and teenagers. As
our members, they can freely use any training course or equipment
of our gym.

My account has expired, can I recover it?

If you received a notification via e-mail or SMS about your account
expiration, you can still recover it during the next 30 days using our
website. If you don’t have a possibility to do it, you can visit our office
to undergo a recovery procedure.

How do I access my account?

You can access your account using our website or follow the link you
receive in registration e-mail. Then you need to enter your
membership card number and the password you received in
registration e-mail. We tried to make the member zone of our site as
comfortable and secured as it can be, equipping it with an intuitive
interface, and we hope you like it.

Not Sure Which Plan is Right For You?

If you are hesitating over a choice of a suitable membership plan, send us a letter
with any questions or suggestions you have.
