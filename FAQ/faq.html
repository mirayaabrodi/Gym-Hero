General Questions

How can i join your group workouts?

To become a part of any group at GYM on a regular basis, you are to
have at least the Starter membership card. However, if you would like to
give a crack at group training, you can visit a free workout. It takes place
every Saturday at our gym and includes elements of several disciplines.

What trainning is the better for me?

It is a difficult question to answer. If you have no previous experience in
training or are trying it for the first time. To achieve the best possible
result during your further training, we recommend our new members to
take part in the free physical test. Afterward, we will pick the most
suitable training course for you.

At what age im allowed to join the gym?

Usually, we accept members aged at 18 and higher, but there are some
exceptions for teenagers and children under 13 years old. For these
membership categories, we have special group training, aimed at the
development of basic physical skills including endurance and muscular
power.

Can i refer a friend to your gym?

Sure you can. We always encourage our members to involve their
friends in training and sports activities. According to the rules &
regulations of our gym, your friend or family member can take part in
one workout. If your friend decides to join us, you can get an annual
membership discount.

Are there any Bounuses included with the membership?

We have the loyalty program for our customers. It allows our members
to receive additional bonuses according to their training program and
membership. The most popular and frequent bonuses are discounts on
various workouts and annual membership. Contact our managers to find
out more.

Can i pay by cash or Card?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, and American Express cards, but you can
also pay in cash for any of our services. Our group packages and
membership plans are usually paid by card. However, you can also pay
using your PayPal account via our website. If you have any questions,
feel free to contact our support.

First Workout - Free

You can easily try our services for free, and decide which workout is
better for you. Just let us know what training you prefer.
